My Continuing Journey to Independence

Hi there! First, thank you for visiting the site and taking an interest in who I am and what I do. For those of you that may have stumbled upon this site by accident my name is Marc Sze and I study the bacterial microbiome. At the present time I'm best known for the lung microbiome research that I completed at the University of British Columbia. I've recently migrated to the University of Michigan to study stool and GI microbiomes. This section of my site is dedicated to explaining how I got started, got interested in, and ultimately trapped in trying to pursue scientific research as a viable career for the past 8 years and counting. If you could care less about this story feel free to skip ahead and directly access my CV for the point form version.
