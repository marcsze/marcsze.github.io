Possibly One of Many

A Scientist Blog on Life, Work, and Society

"You inherit your environment just as much as your genes" - Johnny Rich, The Human Script

Here is my slice or small area in which I can freely write about my journey through the academic meat grinder. There are things that I love about academia and things that I absolutely despise. This blog is meant as a sounding board for both of these things. It is meant to talk about life work and society in general because in truth all of these components are woven together when science is invovled.

This blog is a documentation of my journey navigating this complexity and all posts are my own opinions. You are more than welcome join along for the ride as I attempt to make sense of all the noise...
