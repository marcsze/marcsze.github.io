Treading in Complexity - A Microbiome Oriented Blog

"You inherit your environment just as much as your genes" - Johnny Rich, The Human Script

In all honesty, I think this quote perfectly summarizes the complexity of studying the bacterial microbiome and how it interacts with it's human host. Just when you think you have it figured out a new variable or confounder pops out at you to throw you back into ignorance. This blog is a documentation of my journey navigating this complexity and all posts are of my own attempts at trying to tread and pop my head out above the unknown....
