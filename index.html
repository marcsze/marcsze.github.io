Treading in Complexity - A Microbiome Oriented Blog

"You inherit your environment just as much as your genes" - Johnny Rich, The Human Script

In all honesty, I think this quote provides a good microcosm for the overall complexity and sometimes maddening frustration that occurs from studying the bacterial microbiome on a daily basis. Just when you think you have it figured out something pops out at you and you realize just how little you truly know.

This blog is a documentation of my journey navigating this complexity and all posts are my own opinions. You are more than welcome along for the ride as I attempt to make sense of all the noise...
