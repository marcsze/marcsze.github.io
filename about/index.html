My Continuing Journey to Independence

Hi there! First, thank you for visiting the site and taking an interest in who I am and what I do. For those of you that may have stumbled upon this site by accident my name is Marc Sze and I study the bacterial microbiome. At the present time I'm best known for the lung microbiome research that I completed at the University of British Columbia. Now I've migrated to stool and GI microbiomes. This section of my site is dedicated to explaining how I got started, get interested in, and ultimately trapped in trying to pursue scientific research for the last 8 years and counting. If you could care less about this story feel free to skip directly to my CV for the point form version.
